Solution

Tesla Optimus robots will be deployed to assist all levels of provider care. The pilot begins with simple tech duties and then moves up the stack to assist nurses and physicians. Robots can do simple manual tasks, and their AI will support more complex cognitive tasks.

Tech Assist

Make beds, prepare suture trays, supply runs.

Nurse Assist

Medication delivery, vitals relay, patient updates.

Physician Assist

History, diagnosis, and workup support.
