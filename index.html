Problem

Staffing shortages

Shrinking RN/tech supply with rising census and coverage gaps.

Burnout from repetitive tasks

High‑volume low‑complexity duties drain focus and morale.

Increasing complexity requiring AI

Case mix, protocols, and documentation outpace human bandwidth—AI support is essential.

See our proposed approach

Solution

Tesla Optimus robots will be deployed to assist all levels of provider care. The pilot begins with simple tech duties and then moves up the stack to assist nurses and physicians. Robots can do simple manual tasks, and their AI will support more complex cognitive tasks.

Tech Assist

Make beds, prepare suture trays, supply runs.

Nurse Assist

Medication delivery, vitals relay, patient updates.

Physician Assist

History, diagnosis, and workup support.

Roadmap

Pre‑Pilot

  • Confirm partnerships with a San Diego ED and with Tesla
  • Establish business case, moat, projections with Advisory Board

Pilot Study

  • Deploy first robot for simple tasks (e.g., clean gurney)
  • Expand tasks (suture trays, intra‑ED transport)
  • Integrate with EMR
  • Explore data input to reduce RN documentation time
  • Explore robot‑patient history taking to assist MDs

Scale

  • Add tasks and robots
  • Deploy to other hospitals
